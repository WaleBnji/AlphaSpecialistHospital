ABOUT US

What We Are!

Alpha Specialist Hospital is a licencsed medical facility situated in Akobo area of Ibadan operating since 2017. We are registered and licensed by relevant regulatory bodies including the NHIS. As a hospital, we have continually evolved in our operations and care service provision for our customers. This led to the recent commissioning of our new, fully equiped ultra-modern hospital affording us the opportunity to serve a wider range of people.

At Alpha Specialist Hospital, we work in compliance with local and international best practices when providing our services. It is our goal that everyone enjoys quality healthcare services at affordable cost.

Our Mission

We are committed to delivering exceptional healthcare services through focus on excellence amd innovation in patient health management, state of the art facilities, research for growth and community development.

Our Vision

To be a leading medical center renowned for providing quality and affordable healthcare services.

OUR PACKAGES

OBSTETRICS AND GYNAECOLOGY

Our dedicated team of obstetricians and gynecologists provides expert care for women at every stage of life, from prenatal and maternity care to reproductive health and gynecological procedures.

INTERNAL MEDICINE

Our internal medicine specialists focus on diagnosing and managing a wide range of adult health concerns, offering personalized treatment plans to address your specific medical needs.

SURGERY

Our skilled surgical team is equipped to perform a variety of surgical procedures, from general surgery to specialized surgeries, ensuring the highest standards of safety and care.

FAMILY PLANNING

We provide comprehensive family planning services, offering counseling, contraception options, and reproductive health guidance to support your family planning goals.

RADIOLOGY

Our state-of-the-art radiology department offers a full spectrum of diagnostic imaging services, including X-rays, MRI, CT scans, and ultrasounds, aiding in accurate diagnosis and treatment planning.

LABORATORY

Our well-equipped laboratory facilitates a wide range of diagnostic tests, allowing for timely and accurate assessment of various medical conditions, contributing to effective patient care.

PAEDIATRICS

Our pediatric care team specializes in providing healthcare services tailored to infants, children, and adolescents, focusing on their growth, development, and well-being.

DIALYSIS

We offer advanced dialysis services for patients with kidney conditions, ensuring they receive the life-saving treatment and support they need to maintain their health.

HAVE QUESTIONS?

Frequently asked questions

When can i see a doctor when I come to the hospital?

Alpha Specialist Hospital run a 24 hour clinic service to all our patients. Anytime you decide to vist ASH, there will always be a doctor ready to attend to your health

I lost my hospital card. Can I get another one?

Please visit our hospital's front desk or the administrative office, where they can assist you in obtaining a replacement card. They will guide you through the process and ensure that you have the necessary documentation. If you have any questions or need further assistance, please feel free to ask, and we'll be happy to help you get your care card sorted out.

Can I see a doctor without my hospital card?

We can verify your identity using alternative forms of identification, such as a photo ID or other personal information. Our priority is your well-being, and we'll work with you to ensure you receive the care you need. Please proceed to the registration desk, and our staff will assist you in the process

Is ambulance available for emergencies?

Oh! Yes! We operate emergency ambulance service to attend to emergency cases anytime and anywhere within ibadan. Any emergency? Please call us at: 0803-ASH-CARE

Can I work at Alpha?

Certainly. However, we are currently not recruiting. We will be sure to put it out there when services are required. Do ensure to apply then.

Can I do CS for delivery?

Alpha Specialist Hospital offers well controlled delivery services that soothes the patients choice. Vaginal delivery with pain control or CS delivery

Can I get a medical report?

As long as you are our patient and you have been attended to and certified by one of our doctors, yes!

IDoes ASH have surgical theatre?

Alpha Specialist Hospital has state of the art theatre rooms where major and minor surgeries are performed following due international standards. To check our facilities, click here: Facilities
